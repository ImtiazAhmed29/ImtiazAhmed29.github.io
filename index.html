Bio

I'm an NYC-based mechanical engineering student at the Cooper Union, and I'm interested in aerospace and robotics. Regarding aerospace specifically, I am interested in aerodynamics, aircraft structural design, and control. I've been particularly curious about the use of sensors in aircraft and robots and how data comes together to create a more complete image of an environment, and how entities respond to these inputs. I'm also interested in mechanical linkages that can function on aircraft or robots as deployment or mobility mechanisms.

I'm currently working at the Loisaida Center in New York City, designing air quality sensor cases for deployment across buildings in the city. I'm also working with the Bio-inspired Design Lab at the Cooper Union, focusing on the manufacturing process for the wings in small flapping-wing micro-air vehicles.
